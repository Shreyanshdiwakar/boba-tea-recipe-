vanilla boba tea

vanilla boba tea with caramel icing.

Ingredients

Cake

  • Boba Pearls
  • Brown Sugar Syrup
  • Black Tea
  • Milk Mixture
  • ¾ cup tapioca flour
  • 3 cups ice
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Directions

    1. Heat water and muscovado sugar in a pan until boiling.
    2. Gradually mix in tapioca flour.
      3. Cook muscovado sugar and water until it reduces into syrup.
    3. Boil the tapioca balls for 20 minutes, then rinse and set aside in brown sugar syrup.
    4. Whisk together half-and-half and sweetened condensed milk.
    5. Whisk together half-and-half and sweetened condensed milk.
    6. Add boba pearls, ice, black tea, and milk mixture to a glass. Stir and serve..
    7. Make icing: Combine brown sugar, butter, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until brown sugar is dissolved, about 3 minutes. Add evaporated milk and continue stirring. Bring to a gentle boil and let bubble for about 4 minutes, stirring constantly to avoid sticking. Remove from heat and allow to cool, about 5 minutes.
    8. Mix confectioners' sugar and vanilla extract into brown sugar mixture using an electric mixer until icing caramelizes and thickens to the desired consistency.
    9. pour icing over boba tea and serve
